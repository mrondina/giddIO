How It Works

Challenge Yourself, Feel Amazing

On giddy, challenges provide the framework to enable individuals and creators to inspire each other and big communities with new product ideas.

There are new challenges launching every month with prizes for those with the best ideas.

Show & Tell

Some say a picture is worth a thousand words. Your entry is worth so much more :) Entering a challenge is a simple 3 step process

  1. Take a picture or video of your idea
  2. Give it a creative title
  3. Add a short description

With these three things, you hit submit and your idea gest shared with the entire giddy community!

Emoji Voting FTW

Provide your emoji reaction on an entry to show the idea creator what you think. The entry with the most emoji from the top category will be crowned the winner of the challenge.
